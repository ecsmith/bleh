Templates×Wrap Media

What is a Wrap?

A "Wrap" is essentially a mobile storytelling tool for delivering bite-sized mobile experiences in a “card-based” format. Wrap's value add as product for a small business is that they can deliver content to their users through a mobile web app that is platform agnostic and allows them to bypass the costly task of hiring a developer.

These our some examples of Wraps in the form of our "Embedded Player". Wraps can live anywhere–in an email for a marketing campaign, as an Advertisement on a site, in an SMS to a friend or just at a plain-old URL.

Template Wraps — Think Squarespace, but for mobile apps

Over the course of 6 months, myself and the rest of the design team created 150+ template wraps for our customers to use. Templates gave our users a starting point in our platform and provided them some inspiration to create their own wraps. One of the biggest challenges in constructing templates was making them easy to use so that a brand/buisiness could simply swap out their content.

Here's a small subset of the templates we created. Here you can see many of the common use cases for using a wrap such as commerce, brand awareness, real estate and events/conferences.
